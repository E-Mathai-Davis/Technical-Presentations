I wish to provide insight into my understanding of the topics that I am studying. One powerful method of doing this is by demonstrating what I am able to teach to someone who is new to the topic. It allows me to present my own personal intuition for the subject matter while prioritizing making that information communicable.

This repository documents the technical presentations that I have created to share my knowledge and intuition for the core techniques and fundamental concepts related to the fields of semiconductors, electronics, and circuit design.
